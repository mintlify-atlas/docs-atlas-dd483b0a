## Welcome to the NJ Rajat Mahotsav Platform

The NJ Rajat Mahotsav platform is a full-featured event website built for the **Shree Swaminarayan Temple Secaucus** Silver Jubilee celebration. It handles everything from public-facing event information to secure admin management of thousands of attendee registrations.

*Rajat Mahotsav* means "Silver Jubilee" in Sanskrit — marking 25 years of the temple's presence in New Jersey. The 2026 celebration runs **July 29 – August 2** and is expected to draw thousands of devotees from across North America.

## About the event

The **Shree Swaminarayan Temple Secaucus** (part of SSSSM USA) was established by Acharya Swamishree Maharaj in 1995 and has served the New Jersey Swaminarayan community for 25 years. The Rajat Mahotsav celebration brings together devotees for five days of cultural programs, religious ceremonies, and community gatherings.
